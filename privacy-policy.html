About PixBud

PixBud is a creative inspiration app that provides curated images and ready-made prompts. Users can explore vibe-based images, view prompt details, copy or share prompts, and open prompts in third-party AI tools such as Google Gemini.

PixBud does not require account registration and does not allow users to upload personal photos or content.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Personal details such as name, phone number, or email address
  • User accounts or login credentials
  • Uploaded photos or files
  • Payment or financial information
  • Precise location data

Automatically Collected Information

To ensure app stability and improve performance, limited non-personal information may be collected automatically, including:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash reports and error logs

This information is anonymous and cannot be used to personally identify users.

Notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging)

PixBud uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send optional notifications, such as updates about new vibes or features.

These notifications do not contain personal data. Firebase may collect limited device-related information, such as a device token, solely for notification delivery. PixBud does not access, store, or use this information for identification purposes.

Notifications can be disabled at any time through your device settings.

How Information Is Used

  • Understand general feature usage trends

PixBud does not sell, rent, or misuse user data.

PixBud is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party platforms.

Internet Requirement

An active internet connection is required to load content and redirect prompts to external AI tools. When an internet connection is unavailable, the app may display an offline message.
